What is the significance of magnitude and depth?

The potential damage of earthqaukes needs consider both magnitude and depth. Generally, earthquakes of magnitude 6 and above can cause shaking intensities when nearby.Regards of depth, the deeper the quake, the less violent and less dangerous. That's because the senergy released felt from an earthquake diminishes with increasing distance from the quake's source.
